The Challenge

Our Cities Are Struggling

🌡️

Urban Heat Islands

Cities are up to 7°C hotter than surrounding rural areas due to concrete surfaces and lack of vegetation. This drives up energy consumption and heat-related health risks.

💨

Air Pollution Crisis

Urban air quality continues to deteriorate. Poor air quality contributes to respiratory diseases, cardiovascular issues, and reduced quality of life for millions.

🏗️

Wasted Rooftop Space

Over 60% of urban rooftop area sits completely unused. This represents a massive, untapped opportunity to green our cities without using additional land.
